EXPLORE CAGAYAN DE ORO CITY

The Heart of Northern Mindanao

Cagayan de Oro, known as the "City of Golden Friendship", is a vibrant and rapidly growing urban center in Northern Mindanao, Philippines. With a rich history and diverse cultural heritage, it serves as the capital of Misamis Oriental and a key economic and educational hub in the region.

Founded in the 17th century and officially chartered as a city in 1950, Cagayan de Oro has evolved into a thriving commercial center supported by robust manufacturing, retail, and service industries. The city's strategic location is enhanced by its international airport and proximity to major highways, making it the gateway to Northern Mindanao.

Renowned for its adventure tourism, particularly white-water rafting on the Cagayan River, the city attracts thrill-seekers from around the world. Cultural highlights include the annual Higalaay Festival, which showcases the city's rich traditions and community spirit.
